[[qry-ListAndSubscribePartitions]]
===== List and subscribe partitions
Lists all non-language partitions accessible in the repository, and subscribes the client to all of them.{fn-org361}
The client receives the full contents of all listed partitions.{fn-org425}

See also <<qry-ListPartitions>>.
