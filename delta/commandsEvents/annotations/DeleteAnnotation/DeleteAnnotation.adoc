[[cmd-DeleteAnnotation]]
===== Delete annotation
Delete <<existing-nodes, existing node>> <<DeleteAnnotation.deletedAnnotation>>{fn-org371} from <<DeleteAnnotation.parent>>'s annotations at <<DeleteAnnotation.index>>, and all its descendants (including annotation instances).
Does NOT change references to any of the deleted nodes.{fn-org285}
