[[evnt-AnnotationReplaced]]
===== Annotation replaced
<<existing-nodes, Existing node>> <<AnnotationReplaced.replacedAnnotation>>, and all its <<AnnotationReplaced.replacedDescendants>>{fn-org370}, inside <<AnnotationReplaced.parent>>'s annotations at <<AnnotationReplaced.index>> has been replaced with <<new-nodes, new>> or <<reused-nodes, reused>> node <<AnnotationReplaced.newAnnotation>>.

<<AnnotationReplaced.replacedAnnotation>>, and all its descendants, have been deleted.
