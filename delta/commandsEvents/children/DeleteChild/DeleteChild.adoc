[[cmd-DeleteChild]]
===== Delete child
Delete existing node <<DeleteChild.deletedChild>>{fn-org371} from <<DeleteChild.parent>>'s <<DeleteChild.containment>> at <<DeleteChild.index>>, and all its descendants (including annotation instances).
Does NOT change references to any of the deleted nodes.{fn-org285}
