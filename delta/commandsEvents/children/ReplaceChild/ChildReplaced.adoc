[[evnt-ChildReplaced]]
===== Child replaced
<<existing-nodes, Existing node>> <<ChildReplaced.replacedChild>>, and all its <<ChildReplaced.replacedDescendants>>{fn-org370}, inside <<ChildReplaced.parent>>'s <<ChildReplaced.containment>> at <<ChildReplaced.index>> has been replaced with <<new-nodes, new>> or <<reused-nodes, reused>> node <<ChildReplaced.newChild>>.

<<ChildReplaced.replacedChild>>, and all its descendants, have been deleted.

.Technical name
`ChildReplaced`

[horizontal]
.Parameters
[[ChildReplaced.newChild, `newChild`]]newChild:: <<singleChunkType>>
Subtree that formed the new child.
The _anchor node_ MUST contain the <<ChildReplaced.parent>> as _parent_.{fn-org357}
All nodes in that subtree MUST be <<new-nodes>> or <<reused-nodes>>.{fn-org326}
