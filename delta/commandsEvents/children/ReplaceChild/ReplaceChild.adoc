[[cmd-ReplaceChild]]
===== Replace child
Delete <<existing-nodes, current child>> <<ReplaceChild.replacedChild>>{fn-org371} inside <<ReplaceChild.parent>>'s <<ReplaceChild.containment>> at <<ReplaceChild.index>>, and all its descendants (including annotation instances).
Does NOT change references to any of the deleted nodes.{fn-org285}

Replace <<existing-nodes, existing node>> <<ReplaceChild.replacedChild>> inside <<ReplaceChild.parent>>'s <<ReplaceChild.containment>> at <<ReplaceChild.index>> with <<new-nodes, new>> or <<reused-nodes, reused>> node <<ReplaceChild.newChild>>.

Subtree to form a new child.
The _anchor node_ MUST contain the <<ReplaceChild.parent>> as _parent_.{fn-org357}
All nodes in that subtree MUST either be <<new-nodes>> or <<reused-nodes>>.
